About Kronosphere

Kronosphere develops and runs computer programming courses and holiday camps for youth, enabling them to become technological creators. We have a simple but audacious mission: to empower the next generation.

We are fueled by our dual passions for teaching and computer programming, and our belief in its criticality as a skill in the near future.
Our programs are designed to be practical, interactive, engaging and fun.

Our drive has taken us to schools in Singapore, Malaysia and India.
And next, the world.
